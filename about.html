About

  • He has worked for several national and international NGOs and United Nations (UN) organizations like UNESCO, UNDP, UNV, and UNCTAD in Africa. And also engaged with some famous global organizations like the International Association of Students in Economics and Commerce (AIESEC) and Junior Chamber International (JCI) where he holds leadership positions in the past. As an Economic Freedom Fighter (EFF), Mr. Coulibaly is also currently supporting the promotion of entrepreneurship among Youth and Women organizations for more than ten (10) years in the issues of information and communication, education and training, technical assistance and consulting, studies and research.
  • In 2011-2012, he served as the Economic and Commercial Counsellor for Africa at the Sichuan Supplier Chamber of Commerce (SSCC) in China and continues to develop the economic, social, and cultural relations between Africa and China. In 2015-2016, he has been selected as one of the UN Women’s Empower Women Program Global Champion for Women’s Economic Empowerment (WEE) where he demonstrated his ability in supporting rural women entrepreneurs. In 2016-2017, he was one of the UN Women’s Empower Women Program Mentor in the Business Development Group (BDG). In 2018, he recently joined the Side By Side Faith Movement for Gender Justice as a key member of its National Steering Group in Mali, the Sub-Regional Steering Group in West Africa, and the Global Steering Group in the United Kingdom (UK). In 2019, he has been appointed as one of the members of the Consultative Council of UN Women in Mali.
  • He obtained an Associate’s Degree in Economics and Development at the University of Bouaké (Côte d’Ivoire), Bachelor’s and Master’s Degrees in Management at the University of Bamako (Mali) in 2000. He also obtained a Postgraduate Diploma in Human Rights from the Centre Universitaire Henry Dunant (CUHD) in Geneva (Switzerland) in 2008, a Certificate in Mineral Policy and Contract Negotiation from the University of Johannesburg (UJ) in South Africa in 2013, and a Certificate in Business and Human Rights in 2016 and a Certificate in Advanced Course in Human Rights on the role of Men and Boys in Achieving Gender Equality in 2018 at the University of Pretoria (UP) in South Africa. In 2020, he obtained the prestigious GenderPro Certificate at the Global Women’s Institute (GWI) of The George Washington University (GWU).
  • Mr. Kassoum Coulibaly speaks both French and English.
