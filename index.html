Welcome to Kassoum Coulibaly Page

Mr. Coulibaly is the Founder, President, and CEO of the Afrika Global Gender Academy (AGGA), a branch of the Afrika Global Group (AG Group) LLC – USA, a member of MenEngage Global Alliance and also the National Coordinator of MenEngage Network Mali and Vice-Chair of the Steering Committee of MenEngage Africa Alliance. He is also a UN Women’s HeForShe Initiative Member and the Co-Founder of the African Development Group (ADG), the African Mining Alliance (AMA), the Alliance for African Renaissance (ARA), and the African Youth Center (AYC).
